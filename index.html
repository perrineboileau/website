What is Musical ?

Musical ESSEC is a musical theater club founded in 1998 by a group of ambitious and passionate students. The project has kept growing ever since and Musical is now one of ESSEC Business School's best-recognized student associations. Each year, a team of 50 students - exclusively in Management, from first to last year - entirely creates and produces an original musical theater play : script, play, songs, lyrics, choreographies, costumes and stage set. The semi-professional productions are put together in 8 months of intense writing, composing and rehearsing.
